1. <span data-ttu-id="cbbda-104">Ihneď [vytvorte nové heslo používateľa](https://support.office.com/article/7a5d073b-7fae-4aa5-8f96-9ecd041aba9c).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cbbda-104">[Reset the user's password](https://support.office.com/article/7a5d073b-7fae-4aa5-8f96-9ecd041aba9c) immediately.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cbbda-105">Neoznamujte nové heslo koncovému používateľovi e-mailom.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cbbda-105">Do not communicate the new password through email to the end user.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="cbbda-107">Odstráňte všetky podozrivé [pravidlá pre doručenú poštu](https://support.office.com/article/1433E3A0-7FB0-4999-B536-50E05CB67FED) nastavené v poštovej schránke.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cbbda-107">Remove any suspicious [inbox rules](https://support.office.com/article/1433E3A0-7FB0-4999-B536-50E05CB67FED) set within the mailbox.</span></span> 
    
4. <span data-ttu-id="cbbda-108">Ak má používateľ zablokované odosielanie e-mailov, [prejdite do časti obmedzených používateľov a konto odblokujte](https://protection.office.com/?hash=/restrictedusers).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cbbda-108">If the user is blocked from sending email, [go to the Restricted Users to unblock the account](https://protection.office.com/?hash=/restrictedusers).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cbbda-109">Po uplynutí jednej hodiny po dokončení by mal používateľ môcť odosielať správy.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cbbda-109">Once done, you should be able to resume sending messages  within 1 hour  .</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="cbbda-111">Do budúcnosti na minimalizovanie rizika úniku údajov alebo zneužitia konta odporúčame prečítať si náš [článok o najvhodnejších postupoch zabezpečenia služieb Office 365](https://support.office.com/article/9295e396-e53d-49b9-ae9b-0b5828cdedc3).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cbbda-111">To minimize the potential of a data breach or a compromised account in the future, we recommend reading our [Office 365 Security best practices article](https://support.office.com/article/9295e396-e53d-49b9-ae9b-0b5828cdedc3).</span></span>
